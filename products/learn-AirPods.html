Air Pods Pro 3

An exceptional spatial listening experience, with high-definition, three-dimensional audio.

New ultra-low-noise microphones

Using advanced computational audio to remove more noise around you than ever, AirPods Pro 3 let you zone in (or out) when you need to. And five all-new ear tips — including an XXS option — fuse a thin layer of soft foam to the silicone shell to give you even better acoustic performance.

Voice Isolation

AirPods Pro 3 reduce background noise and isolate voices during calls. So whether you’re on the bus or in a café, every conversation is loud and clear.15

Adaptive Audio

AirPods Pro 3 combine Active Noise Cancellation with next-level Transparency, which makes your voice and the world around you sound even more natural and vibrant, intelligently adapting to your environment to prioritize what you need to hear, when you need to hear it.

Conversation Awareness

AirPods Pro 3 can detect when you’re talking to someone nearby and automatically lower the volume of whatever you’re listening to. When you’re done, the volume goes right back to where you had it.
