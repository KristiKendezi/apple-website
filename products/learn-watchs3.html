Watch SE 3

A new Always-On displat lets you read the time and more without raising your wrist.

Track your daily health status in the Vitals app

Quickly see your overnight health data right on your wrist — including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration — and get notified if your metrics are ever outside your typical range.

Get retrospective ovulation estimates

SE 3 has advanced sensors that can track your wrist temperature while you sleep.1 The Cycle Tracking app uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning

Know your sleep score

Sleep quality is influenced by factors such as sleep duration, bedtime consistency, how often you wake up, and time spent in each sleep stage. Sleep score analyzes these factors every night and provides a classification and a score. You’ll see how the score is calculated, so you can understand the quality of your sleep and learn how to make it more restorative.

Get a pulse check with the Heart Rate app.

Check your heart rate at any time with the Heart Rate app. And get notifications for a high or low heart rate or an irregular rhythm.

Your go‑to Health app

The Health app helps you organize your important health information in one central and secure place. It also provides interactive charts and advanced trend analysis, so you can view your health data over time.
