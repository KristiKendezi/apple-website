Watch Ultra 3

Built for athletes and adventures for every kind.

Hit the ground running with the Action button

It’s customizable and gives you quick control over a variety of functions, so you can do things like control a workout, move to your next interval, mark a segment, and more. All with extreme precision.

All you need is your watch

Take and make calls, download music and podcasts, and pay for your post-run latte on the go.8 And thanks to a new 5G modem and antenna architecture, Ultra 3 provides better coverage in areas with weak reception.

Advance with advanced metrics

Track your progress with metrics like running power and cadence. And if you create a Custom Workout on your watch or iPhone, you can add work and recovery intervals, set a pace for outdoor workouts, and more. You can even get an estimate of your VO2 max after your run.

Track your training load

Helpful insights from training load show the impact your workouts have on your body over time, so it’s easier to plan when to push and when to rest — especially useful when you’re training for your next big event.

Monitor your Heart Rate Zones

Quickly see your exercise intensity level with Heart Rate Zones, which are automatically calculated and personalized using your health data. You can adjust them manually or set up alerts.

Run with Race Route and Pacer.

Race Route allows you to race against your last or best time on your favorite runs. Pacer lets you pick a distance and time target for an outdoor run and tells you when you’re on or off track.
